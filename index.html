About Dr. Jorge Kim

Dr. Jorge Kim is double board certified in Pain Medicine and Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation.

He graduated from the University of California Los Angeles (UCLA) and then went on to complete his medical training at the University of California at Davis. After, he completed his Anesthesiology Pain fellowship at the prestigious University of California Irvine.

Dr. Kim worked as the Chief of Pain Medicine for the largest county hospital in Alameda County and then worked in McKinney, Texas for several years. He now brings over 20 years of medical training to help his patients and community.

From a long track record of success, he has a unique perspective dealing with acute and chronic pains. He understands that every patient has their own story to tell. Through listening and empathizing, he works with his patients to provide the most up-to-date and evidence-based treatments to reduce pain and promote recovery.
